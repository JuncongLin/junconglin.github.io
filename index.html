About Me

Juncong Lin is is currently a full professor in the software school of Xiamen University, leading the Graphics and Virtual Reality Laboratory. He received his B.S. and Ph.D Degree both from Zhejiang Univesity in 2003 and 2008 respectively. Before joining Xiamen University, he has worked in Chinese University of Hong Kong, JST ERATO IGARASHI Design Interface Project, Nanyang Technological University as a postdoc researcher. His research interests include shape modeling with creativity support, sketch based shape modeling and comic content processing. He has published over 20 papers in venus including ACM SIGCHI, IEEE TVCG. He has been principle investigators of many fundings including National Key Technology R&D Program Foundation of China, National Natural Science Foundation of China, Joint Funds of the Ministry of Education of China and China Mobile etc. He has been enrolled in Program for New Century Excellent Talents in University of Fujian, High Level Oversea Talent of Xiamen. He has received several awards including Luzengyong CAD&CG High Tech Aen Airline Teaching Award, Excellent Supervisor of Intel Cup National Collegiate Software Innovation Contest.

Research Interests

My research interests fall into the general areas of computer graphics and virtual reality. I'm particularly interested in gemoetric modeling and related user interaction. Currently I am interested in the following topics:
  • Exploratory Urban Modeling
  • Sketch based Modeling
  • Head Mounted Display
  • Shape Analysis 3D Printing
  • Creativity Support Freeform Object Modeling
